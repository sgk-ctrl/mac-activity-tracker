# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) v2.1.

In short: be respectful, welcoming, and constructive. Harassment, discrimination,
and personal attacks are not tolerated. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject
contributions and comments that violate this code, and may ban contributors for
serious or repeated violations.

Report unacceptable behavior privately to **sandheep@gmail.com**. Reports will be
reviewed and handled confidentially.
